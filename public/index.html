Welcome to SnapNotes

Authenticate with Cognito, upload images to S3 via pre-signed URLs, and store searchable notes in DynamoDB.

Get started Upload View notes

How it works

  1. Sign up & log in to get a Cognito access token.
  2. Request a pre-signed S3 URL and upload directly from the browser.
  3. Create a note with the S3 key, caption, and tags.
  4. Search & download your notes anytime.
